Daman Sidhu, MSc

Psychometrist

Daman Sidhu is a psychometrist with a strong academic and clinical background in psychology and neuroscience. She holds an Honours Bachelor of Science from the University of Toronto, a Graduate Certificate in Brain Disorder Management from Mohawk College, and a Master of Science in Psychology and Neuroscience of Mental Health from King's College London.

Daman has extensive experience administering psychoeducational and developmental assessments to children, adolescents, and adults presenting with a range of neurodevelopmental, learning, and emotional concerns. She has worked in both clinical and research settings, using a wide range of standardized assessment tools to support diagnostic clarification and educational planning.

Known for her calm and thoughtful approach, Daman is committed to creating a positive assessment experience for clients and families. She strives to ensure that each evaluation is not only accurate and comprehensive, but also meaningful and empowering.
