Educational

Services Offered by our Educational Therapist & Consultant:

With over 26 years of experience in education, our educational therapist provides individualized, strategic support to students and families navigating the education system—from accessing school-based supports to preparing for highly competitive post-secondary programs. She combines compassion, deep system knowledge, and practical tools to help students thrive.

Whether you're navigating the complexities of special education, planning next steps after high school, or preparing for a competitive university application—our mission is to walk alongside your family with compassion, clarity, and unwavering advocacy

University & College Application Guidance

  • End-to-end support for post-secondary applications in Ontario, across Canada, the U.S., U.K., and Australia
  • Expert guidance on application strategy, timelines, and program selection
  • Editing and coaching for personal statements, essays, and written responses
  • Interview preparation and coaching for video submissions and virtual interviews
  • Insight into what competitive programs are looking for—based on years of insider knowledge

Supplemental Application & Essay Coaching

  • Advice on extra curricular or volunteer involvement in grade 11 to ensure impact and a competitive edge for supplemental applications
  • Specialized support for competitive programs that require additional application components
  • Brainstorming, writing, and refining personal statements and supplementary materials
  • Preparation and Coaching for video interviews and presentation-based submissions
  • Clear, personalized feedback to help students present their authentic strengths and stand out

IEP Navigation & Special Education Advocacy

  • Expert support understanding and navigating the special education system
  • Review and translation of psychoeducational assessments into actionable strategies
  • Advocacy for accommodations and services students are entitled to
  • Attend ISRC and IPRC as educational advocate
  • Preparation for school meetings, IEP reviews, and accountability conversations
  • Empowering families to speak up—and ensuring schools follow through

High School Course Planning

  • Strategic planning to optimize the "Top 6" average for university admission
  • Ensuring all program prerequisites are met and course loads are balanced
  • Long-term planning from Grade 9 onward to support academic and career goals
  • Advising on program streaming, course changes, and post-secondary alignment

Career Exploration & Pathway Planning

  • Identifying individual strengths, interests, and values through inventories and consultation
  • Exploring career possibilities and matching them with suitable university or college programs
  • Practical insight into job market trends, co-op options, and future-focused pathways
  • Building a personalized post-secondary plan that fits the student—not the other way around
  • Ensuring prerequisites met in secondary school for post secondary planning

Executive Functioning & Academic Coaching

  • One-on-one coaching in time management, organization, and study strategies
  • Building routines, task initiation, and sustained focus
  • Ideal for students with ADHD, learning differences, high performing athletes or those needing structure and accountability
  • Coaching tailored to foster independence, motivation, and academic resilience
