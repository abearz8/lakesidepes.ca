Julie Moscato, MA, RP

Registered Psychotherapist (Qualifying)

Julie Moscato is a Registered Psychotherapist (Qualifying) with over 10 years of experience in the public sector in areas of community safety and crime prevention. Julie has nearly five years' experience working as a mental health practitioner advocating for and working with clients to address their mental health needs.

Julie walks alongside individuals on their therapeutic journeys as they work through experiences of anxiety, pressure, overwhelm, imposter syndrome, self-doubt, lowered self-worth, depression, empty nest syndrome, grief, trauma, interpersonal violence, and relationship challenges.

Julie holds a Master's degree in Counselling Psychology from Yorkville University, a Master's degree in Criminology from the University of Toronto, and an Honours Bachelor of Arts in Psychology from Brock University.

Julie is certified as a Non-Vocational Biblical Counsellor with the Association of Biblical Counsellors. Julie also completed a Brief & Narrative Therapy Certificate Program with the Windz Centre.

Julie's modalities of practice include narrative therapy, Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT), and person-centred therapy, with focuses on mindfulness and self-compassion.

Julie firmly believes in the importance of the therapeutic relationship in achieving positive outcomes. Julie takes an equal and empowering approach with clients, whom she sees as the experts in their own lives. Julie works from a position of non-judgment and humility to create a comfortable environment with clients, where they feel safe sharing.
