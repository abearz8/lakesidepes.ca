Services

Treatment

We provide individual and family therapy for a wide range of presenting conditions. We treat children, adolescents, families and adults.

Our Approach Includes:

  • Cognitive-Behavioural Therapy (CBT)
  • Mindfulness and ACT
  • Emotion-Focused Therapy
  • Family Systems
  • Attachment based Framework
  • Solution-Focused Therapy

Common Presenting Concerns Include:

  • Depressive Disorders (Major Depressive Disorder, Dysthymia)
  • ADHD
  • Parenting and Family Counselling
  • Health Concerns such as chronic illness, chronic pain, and needle phobia
  • Anxiety Disorders (Generalized Anxiety Disorder, Obsessive Compulsive Disorder, Specific Phobias, Social Anxiety and Selective Mutism)

Process:

  • New patients complete a self referral by contacting us through email
  • The initial session is used to get to know you or your child. This usually involves gathering information to help obtain a better understanding of your concerns

Assessments

We provide Psycho-educational Assessments involving a comprehensive assessment of cognitive, academic and social-emotional functioning.

To Identify and Diagnose:

  • Learning Disabilities
  • Giftedness
  • Attention-Deficit / Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD)
  • Anxiety Disorders
  • Depressive Disorders

Generate Specific Treatment Recommendations:

  • Comprehensive recommendations are provided for the school and home
  • Accommodation and remediation strategies are tailored to the child's specific needs, taking into account their individual profile of strengths and weaknesses
  • Our assessment reports can be used to assist in the development of Individual Education Plans (IEP) at school

Process:

  • Complete a self referral by contacting us
  • Assessments usually span 4-5 sessions, including an intake with parents, 2-3 assessment sessions about 2-3 hours in length, a final feedback session. A final report detailing the findings and recommendations will be provided during the feedback meeting.
