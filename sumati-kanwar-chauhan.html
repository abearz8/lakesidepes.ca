Sumati (Sumi) Kanwar Chauhan, Ph.D.

Psychological Assesment Consultant

Sumati holds a doctoral degree in Psychology and has extensive experience in the field of mental health. She conducts psychological, psychoeducational, and mental health assessments for individuals of all ages, including children and adults. Sumati has received specialized training to work with children and adolescents with Autism Spectrum Disorder and other developmental disabilities. For over two decades, Sumati has provided assessment services to individuals struggling with anxiety, depression, behavioral difficulties, ADHD, learning difficulties, and other common issues in young people. She has experience administering and scoring various psychological tests, such as ADOS, ADI-R, WISC, WPPSI, WAIS, WIAT, ABAS, BDI, and BASC.

Sumati is a meticulous professional who is committed to delivering high-quality services to her clients. She is passionate about promoting mental health and well-being, and her expertise has helped her establish a solid reputation in the field. In her leisure time, Sumati enjoys traveling to new places, cooking, and gardening.
