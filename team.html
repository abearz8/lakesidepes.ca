Our Team

Welcome to Lakeside Psychological and Educational Services in Burlington Ontario, where your mental health and well-being are our top priorities! Our dedicated team of clinicians is highly trained and experienced, ensuring you receive the best possible care through evidence-based treatment approaches. We strive to create a warm and welcoming environment, where our clients can feel safe and understood. If you're unsure which psychologist or therapist is the right fit for you, don't worry. Send us an Email or give us a call, and we'll help you find the perfect match to meet your unique needs.
