Treatment

We provide therapy for a wide range of presenting conditions. We treat children, adolescents, families and adults:

Common Presenting Concerns

  • Depressive Disorders (Major Depressive Disorder, Dysthymia)
  • Perinatal Depression
  • ADHD
  • Health Concerns such as chronic illness, chronic pain, and needle phobia
  • Anxiety Disorders (Generalized Anxiety Disorder, Obsessive Compulsive Disorder, Specific Phobias, Social Anxiety and Selective Mutism)

Our Approach Includes

  • Cognitive-Behavioural Therapy (CBT)
  • Mindfulness and ACT
  • Emotion-Focused Therapy
  • Family Systems
  • Attachment based Framework
  • Solution-Focused Therapy

Our Treatment Process

  1. New patients complete a self-referral by contacting us through email
  2. The initial session is used to get to know you or your child. This usually involves gathering information to help obtain a better understanding of your concerns

Individual Psychotherapy

Individual psychotherapy is a talk-based treatment focused on enhancing and sustaining mental health and overall well-being. When working with children, play is often an integral part of the therapeutic process. At Lakeside Psychological and Educational Services, our clinicians collaborate closely with each client to support meaningful change in areas such as thoughts, emotions, behaviors, learning, and social interactions.

We offer individual psychotherapy for children, adolescents, and adults.

Parenting Support

At times, parenting can feel overwhelming, confusing, or isolating. Whether you're navigating everyday challenges, major transitions, or concerns about your child's behavior or development, parenting therapy offers a space to reflect, learn, and grow in your role as a caregiver. Through one-on-one or joint sessions, our clinicians work with parents to:

  • Strengthen communication and connection with their children
  • Manage behavioral or emotional concerns
  • Understand developmental stages and age-appropriate expectations
  • Build confidence in decision-making and parenting style
  • Co-parent effectively through separation, divorce, or blended family dynamics
  • Reduce stress and improve overall family functioning

Parenting therapy is not about judgment—it's about empowering you with insight, tools, and support tailored to your unique family.

Family Therapy

Challenges within a family—whether seen as individual struggles or shared concerns—often benefit from a collective approach. Our family therapy services aim to support open, constructive dialogue that draws on the strengths, insights, and resilience of the entire family.

Family therapy can address a wide variety of issues that impact family dynamics. Whether the concerns feel big or small, personal or shared, working together in a supportive environment can lead to stronger relationships and a healthier, more connected home life.

Executive Functioning Coaching

Our Executive Functioning Coaching is designed to support individuals of all ages in developing the essential cognitive skills needed to thrive at school, at work, and in everyday life. Whether you're a student, professional, or navigating life transitions, our coaching provides personalized strategies to help you succeed.

Areas of Focus Include:

  • Time management and planning
  • Organization and task initiation
  • Focus and attention
  • Goal setting and follow-through
  • Emotional regulation and stress management
  • Self-monitoring and accountability
